About Bahrain Polytechnic

Founded in 2008 as part of Bahrain's Vision 2030, Bahrain Polytechnic is a government-owned institution dedicated to providing high-quality technical and vocational education. Through hands-on learning and industry partnerships, the Polytechnic aims to equip graduates with skills directly relevant to the workforce, supporting Bahrain's economic diversification.

Our Vision, Mission, and Values

Vision: To become a leading institution for applied learning and innovation, positively influencing the future of education and industry.

Mission: Bahrain Polytechnic is committed to developing graduates with professional skills and knowledge aligned with current industry needs, enhancing their employability and fostering a robust labor market.

Values: We prioritize integrity, innovation, respect, and excellence, fostering a culture of continuous learning and ethical responsibility in all we do.

Academic Programs

Bahrain Polytechnic offers a range of programs across disciplines such as Business, Information and Communications Technology (ICT), Engineering, Logistics, and Creative Media. Each program emphasizes a project-based learning model, providing students with practical experience and opportunities to engage with industry through internships, capstone projects, and training programs.

Notable programs include the Bachelor of Engineering Technology, focusing on fields like Mechanical and Electrical Engineering, and the Bachelor of International Logistics Management, which prepares students for roles in global logistics and supply chain management.

State-of-the-Art Facilities

Bahrain Polytechnic's campus in Isa Town is designed to provide students with a modern learning environment. Facilities include dedicated labs for engineering, computing, and creative media, alongside industry-standard resources such as the Innovation Lab for ICT and the Career and Employment Centre, which connects students with job placement and internship opportunities.

The library, equipped with digital resources and collaborative study areas, supports students' research and study needs. Future expansions aim to further enhance the campus with environmentally friendly buildings and technology-integrated spaces to support an innovative learning experience.

Student Life

Student life at Bahrain Polytechnic is vibrant and diverse, with numerous clubs, societies, and events held throughout the year. The Student Council plays an active role in organizing activities that foster a sense of community and cultural awareness, while the campus facilities support sports, arts, and social events.

Students are encouraged to participate in extracurricular activities to develop leadership and teamwork skills. From career fairs to international collaboration projects, Bahrain Polytechnic offers a holistic educational experience that prepares students not just academically but also personally and socially.
