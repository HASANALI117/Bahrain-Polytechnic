Our Story

Summary of the Work Completed for This Project

The project involved creating a webpage for "Our Story" of Bahrain Polytechnic, a higher education institution. The objective was to design a visually appealing webpage that tells the institution’s story and serves as part of a larger website. This included designing the HTML structure, applying CSS styles, and ensuring responsiveness across devices. The navigation bar was designed to provide easy access to other sections like "Home," "About Us," "Academics," and "Admission." The content focused on narrating the journey of Bahrain Polytechnic's founders, highlighting their challenges and vision for combining technology with creativity. External resources like Google Fonts and Font Awesome were integrated to enhance the page’s design. The page was styled with custom CSS to align with Bahrain Polytechnic’s branding guidelines. Responsive design principles were implemented to ensure the page displayed well across desktops, tablets, and phones.

Challenges Faced and How They Were Overcome

The main challenges during the development included ensuring cross-device compatibility, integrating external libraries, and maintaining design consistency. Responsive design was addressed by using flexible layout structures and media queries. We also tested the page on various devices to ensure usability across platforms. The integration of Google Fonts and Font Awesome was simplified by correctly placing the links and verifying integrity policies. The navigation structure was streamlined to provide clarity and improve user navigation. For typography, we chose a small set of Google Fonts for readability and visual harmony. Lastly, content was formatted to improve readability with appropriate headings and spacing.
