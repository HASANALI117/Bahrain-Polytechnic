Admission

Your journey begins here!

To our applicants who wish to join the Polytechnic,

We are pleased to welcome you to Bahrain Polytechnic, where you will acquire the necessary skills and experiences to meet the needs of the labour market.

The admission team is the first department responsible for your entry to the Polytechnic. On this page, we will provide you with all the information related to admissions, as well as the required rules, regulations, and procedures starting from the application itself to confirming your acceptance.

The selection of applicants to Bahrain Polytechnic is very competitive, and this is evident by the increasing demand to study at the Polytechnic over the years due to its great reputation in producing graduates that compete in the labour market.

Once again, welcome to Bahrain Polytechnic! We have high hopes for you, and we believe in your ability to achieve at the Polytechnic just like our previous graduates. We look forward to having you study with us.

We ask God to grant you success in completing your educational journey.
